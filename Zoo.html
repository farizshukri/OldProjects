About Us

Welcome to WildZone Zoo, where the wild meets the wonder of the 90s! Nestled in the heart of Retroville, our zoo has been a favorite destination since opening its gates in 1992. We are committed to conservation, education, and the preservation of wildlife.

Exhibits

Dinosaur Kingdom:
Step back to the prehistoric era with life-size animatronic dinosaurs!

Tropical Rainforest:
Encounter exotic birds, monkeys, and lush greenery in our indoor rainforest habitat.

Savannah Adventure:
Meet the majestic lions, elephants, and giraffes in our open-air Savannah exhibit.

Events

90s Flashback Weekends:
Join us every first Saturday of the month for 90s-themed events, music, and costume contests!

Educational Programs:
WildZone Zoo offers school field trips and educational programs for all ages. Contact us to schedule your group visit!
